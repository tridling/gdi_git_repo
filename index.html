What is StoryKeepers?

StoryKeepers is an open-source project with contributions by social workers, hospice volunteers, and anyone with an interest in preserving the stories of the dying. We are gathering ideas for life reviews, ethical wills, and other legacy activities whose goal is not only preserve the wisdom and stories of those who are approaching the end of their life, but to help them live their last days to the fullest by reflecting, feeling heard, and making peace with the days they have lived.

Whether you are gathering stories from an elderly family member, offer companionship to the dying as a hospice volunteer, or are a professional in healthcare or social work - we invite you to contribute ideas for others to get inspiration from or share your stories of how legacy work has made an impact in your life.
